The Venetian Las Vegas

This majestic resort transports guests to the romantic streets of Venice with its meticulously recreated architecture and gondola rides along the Grand Canal. Boasting one of the largest casinos in the world, The Venetian offers an extensive array of table games, slots, and poker rooms. The opulent suites, upscale shopping at the Grand Canal Shoppes, and gourmet dining options further elevate the guest experience.

Bellagio

Renowned for its iconic dancing fountains and elegant ambiance, the Bellagio is a symbol of sophistication on the Las Vegas Strip. Its opulent casino floor features high-limit tables, slot machines, and a renowned poker room. Guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of the Bellagio Conservatory and Botanical Gardens, indulge in fine dining at Michelin-starred restaurants like Picasso and Le Cirque, or witness breathtaking performances at the O Theatre.

MGM Grand

With its grandiose facade and sprawling complex, the MGM Grand is a Las Vegas institution. The colossal casino floor offers a diverse range of gaming options, including blackjack, roulette, craps, and an expansive race and sports book. The resort also houses the MGM Grand Garden Arena, where guests can catch world-class concerts and sporting events. From the electrifying nightlife at Hakkasan Nightclub to the immersive dining experiences at restaurants like Joel Robuchon and Morimoto, the MGM Grand epitomizes Vegas extravagance.

Wynn Las Vegas

Exuding elegance and sophistication, Wynn Las Vegas sets the standard for luxury on the Strip. Its sleek casino floor features a wide selection of table games and slots, catering to both novice players and seasoned gamblers. Guests can unwind at the serene Wynn Golf Club, rejuvenate at the Forbes Five-Star spa, or savor culinary masterpieces at restaurants like SW Steakhouse and Mizumi. The resort's dazzling nightlife scene, highlighted by the renowned XS Nightclub, ensures that the excitement never wanes.

Caesars Palace

Steeped in Roman-inspired grandeur, Caesars Palace is synonymous with luxury and indulgence. Its expansive casino floor offers an array of gaming options, from classic table games to state-of-the-art slots. Guests can bask in the opulence of the Garden of the Gods Pool Oasis, indulge in world-class shopping at The Forum Shops, or experience unforgettable performances at The Colosseum. With acclaimed restaurants such as Restaurant Guy Savoy and Nobu, Caesars Palace caters to the most discerning palates.

We encourage first-timers to set a budget for gambling and stick to it, take advantage of player rewards programs offered by the hotels, familiarize themselves with the rules of the games they want to play, and explore all the non-gambling attractions that Las Vegas has to offer.

Are there age restrictions for staying at casino hotels in Las Vegas?

Yes, guests must be at least 21 years old to book a hotel room and gamble at casino hotels in Las Vegas. Minors are not allowed on the gaming floor, but some hotels offer family-friendly amenities and activities.
