What makes Las Vegas entertainment scene so special?

Las Vegas is world renowned for their luxurious accommodations, world-class entertainment, comedy shows and extensive casino gaming options. They offer a unique blend of excitement, luxury, and entertainment that is unmatched anywhere else in the world.

What are some tips for first-time visitors to the Las Vegas entertainment secene?

Take advantage of player rewards programs on offer, familiarize themselves with the rules of the games they want to play, and explore all the non-gambling attractions that Las Vegas has to offer. We encourage all visitors to set a budget before taking part in gambling making this an enjoyable and informative experience.

Is there an age limit?

Yes, guests must be at least 21 years old to gamble at casino hotels in Las Vegas. Minors are not allowed on the gaming floor, but some hotels offer family-friendly amenities and activities.
